## 6. Pain Points & Opportunity Drivers
- Lengthy manual processing of applications and permits
- Low transparency leading to citizen dissatisfaction
- Data silos across agencies hindering case coordination
- High administrative overhead and paperwork
- Difficulty tracking policy outcomes and impact

## 7. AI Opportunity Map
| AI Use‑Case | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Citizen Service Assistant | Conversational AI handling FAQs, application status, document guidance |
| Case Management Automation | AI triage to classify and route cases to appropriate units |
| Knowledge Assistant | RAG system for policy, regulation, and procedural information |
| Predictive Resource Allocation | Forecast staffing needs based on workload patterns |
| Fraud & Abuse Detection | Detect fraudulent claims or subsidy misuse |
| Public Sentiment Analysis | NLP on citizen feedback to identify service gaps |

## 8. Common AI Use Cases (Detailed)
1. **Citizen Service Assistant** – chatbot provides instant answers, reduces call‑center volume.
2. **Case Triage Engine** – NLP extracts key entities from applications, routes to correct department.
3. **Policy Knowledge Base** – RAG assistant helps caseworkers retrieve relevant statutes.
