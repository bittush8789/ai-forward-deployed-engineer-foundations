## 8. Common AI Use Cases (Detailed)
1. **Recommendation Engine** – collaborative filtering + content‑based models deliver personalized product lists.
2. **Inventory Forecasting** – time‑series models predict SKU demand at store and warehouse levels.
3. **Dynamic Pricing** – reinforcement learning adjusts prices to maximize margin while maintaining sales volume.
