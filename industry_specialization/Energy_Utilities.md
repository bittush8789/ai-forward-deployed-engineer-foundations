|------|------------|

| NERC | North American Electric Reliability Corporation – reliability standards |

- Legacy SCADA systems lacking analytics capabilities

## 8. Common AI Use Cases (Detailed)
1. **Grid Load Forecasting** – time‑series models incorporate weather forecasts to predict hourly demand.
2. **Predictive Asset Maintenance** – vibration and temperature sensor analytics forecast transformer failures.
3. **Outage Management AI** – combines weather, line health, and historical outage data to prioritize repair crews.
