## 8. Common AI Use Cases (Detailed)
1. **Clinical Documentation Assistant** – records physician dictation, generates structured SOAP notes.
2. **Claims Automation Engine** – extracts billing codes, validates against payer rules.
3. **Population Health Risk Stratifier** – predicts readmission risk for targeted interventions.
