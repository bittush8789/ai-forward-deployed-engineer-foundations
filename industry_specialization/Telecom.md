## 6. Pain Points & Opportunity Drivers
- High churn due to service issues
- Manual fault diagnosis leading to long MTTR
- Complex tariff structures hindering personalized offers
- Revenue leakage from billing errors
- Limited real‑time visibility into network health

## 8. Common AI Use Cases (Detailed)
1. **Network Operations Copilot** – ingest alarm data, logs, and topology to suggest root‑cause analyses.
2. **Churn Prediction** – use usage, complaint, and payment data to score churn risk and trigger offers.
3. **Revenue Assurance** – anomaly detection on billing records to flag under‑/over‑charging.
