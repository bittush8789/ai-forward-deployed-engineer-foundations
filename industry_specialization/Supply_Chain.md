## 8. Common AI Use Cases (Detailed)
1. **Supply Chain Control Tower** – aggregates data from ERP, TMS, WMS, external sources (weather, ports) to provide proactive alerts.
2. **AI Route Optimization** – solves vehicle routing problem with traffic, fuel, and delivery windows.
3. **Demand Forecasting** – ensemble models incorporating promotions, seasonality, and macro‑economic indicators.
