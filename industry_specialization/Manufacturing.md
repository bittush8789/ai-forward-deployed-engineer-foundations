## 8. Common AI Use Cases (Detailed)
1. **Predictive Maintenance Advisor** – ingests vibration, temperature, and usage logs to predict failures with lead time.
2. **AI‑Driven Quality Inspection** – vision models detect surface defects, misalignments, and dimensional errors.
3. **Production Scheduling Optimizer** – reinforcement learning balances machine capacity, labor, and order priorities.
